The HP laptop is a simple laptop. It has a black and grey color scheme that is easy on the eyes. It is fast enough to play Fortnite once in a while when I want to procrastinate. The enter key is a little soft now from spilling some food onto the keyboard; the type of food is no longer remembered. This should be enough text now.

The hole punch on my desk is actually very cool. It is a THREE HOLE PUNCH! This means I can take a sheet of paper and hole punch it so that it will fit into a binder. The Future is now! If you are strong enough, you can also take a thin stack of about 20 pages or so and hole punch them all at once. This has saved me an extra four hours a day that I used to spend using my one hole punch one sheet at a time. This holepunch has changed my life. I wouldn't have passed College 101 without its time saving powers for hole punching blank sheets of paper to put into my binders to hold the printer paper. Wow!

I found a quarter today. It was face up, which means 25 days of good luck followed by three non-consecutive days of medium luck followed by five Thursdays of bad luck. So I was clearly very happy. But, my whole day was ruined when I turned it over to discover that this was a Delaware Quarter! Terrible! I'm sure you all know what a Delaware Quarter Means, so I won't discuss it here. Needless to say I said my seven Hail Wisconsin's, and walked away on the heels of my feet.
